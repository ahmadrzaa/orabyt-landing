Building simple
web & AI solutions for small teams.

ORABYT is a small remote studio from Bahrain & India. We build websites, e-commerce and AI agents in our free time outside our day jobs, and keep the scope honest and practical.

We are registered as an MSME under the Government of India (Udyam), and run as a lean studio no big office, no sales layer just a few people who like shipping real projects end-to-end.

The team

We’re a small group of engineers and collaborators with experience in large organisations (including ex-TCS and ex-IBM background) and hands-on startup work. Everything runs remote Bahrain & India but we stay reachable on Zoom, WhatsApp, email and calls for clients who need ongoing support.
