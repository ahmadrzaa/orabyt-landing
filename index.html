What we do

End to end web, apps & AI built to scale

ORABYT is a remote build studio. You brief us online and we ship production-ready websites, apps and AI experiences without you hiring an in-house team.

  • Websites in any style: corporate, portfolio, blog, property, restaurant, clinic or service sites.
  • E-commerce & delivery: product catalogues, carts, checkout, coupons, food & grocery delivery flows.
  • AI chatbots & agents: website & WhatsApp bots for leads, FAQs, product search and basic support.
  • Dashboards & internal tools: admin panels, approval flows, reports, roles & permissions.
  • Multi-language builds: Arabic, English and other languages with proper RTL/LTR layouts.
  • Integrations & automation: payments, POS, inventory, email/SMS, webhooks and background jobs.

Who builds it: a small senior team (AI engineer, full-stack developer and software lead) working remotely with you startup studio, enterprise experience.

FAQ

Why Orabyt for small business?
We’re a Bahrain & India team building fast sites with built-in AI assistants. Design, hosting, SSL, analytics, and support in one place.
Are you new? What experience do you have?
Founders are ex-TCS & ex-IBM with delivery across web, cloud, and AI. We ship and maintain production systems.
Can I keep my current domain and email?
Yes we link your domain, set up SSL, and route email via Google/Microsoft.
Do you support Arabic & RTL?
Yes bilingual Arabic/English with RTL layout; the AI is trained on both.
How does the AI assistant work?
We feed your menus, PDFs, and policies. The assistant answers, captures leads, and can push to WhatsApp (Pro).
How fast can we go live?
Starter sites launch in 1–2 weeks. Growth & custom move in phases with weekly demos.
