Small Studio. Real Projects.

Orabyt studio • November 2025
ORABYT Client
“We started ORABYT as a small remote studio from Bahrain and India. We work beside our full-time jobs, but treat every project like a real product: clear scope, simple tech, and honest delivery.”

Background

ORABYT is a new remote-first studio registered as an MSME (Udyam) in India. The team has experience in large IT companies (including ex-TCS and ex-IBM) and full-stack work across web, mobile and AI.

Because the studio is lean, we focus on small, high-leverage projects: one website, one AI assistant, one integration that actually goes live instead of a huge system that never ships.

  • Remote only no physical office, low overhead.
  • Work done legally under the registered company.
  • Side-income studio alongside full-time jobs.
  • Hands-on engineering: web, apps, APIs and AI agents.

The challenge

A small business (for example a restaurant, personal brand or property agent) needs a clean website and a basic AI assistant, but:

  • They do not want a heavy CMS or complex dashboard.
  • They need multiple languages (Arabic / English, etc.).
  • They want simple pricing and clear communication.
  • They prefer WhatsApp and phone over complicated support systems.

What we actually build

For this kind of client, ORABYT keeps the setup small and practical.

  1. Pick the right stack. We can ship in Python, Node.js/Express, Next.js, Reactjs, PHP, WordPress or plain HTML/CSS depending on what the client already has and who will maintain it.
  2. Design a simple structure. Typical pages: Home, About, Services / Menu / Properties, Contact / Booking, plus a simple blog or updates page if needed.
  3. Add an AI assistant. A small chatbot trained on the website content and FAQs to answer common questions and collect leads (name, number, interest).
  4. Connect to real channels. For complex questions, the bot hands over to WhatsApp, email or a human directly instead of pretending to know everything.

The result: simple, shippable projects

Because the studio is small and remote, clients talk directly to the people who design and build. There is no sales layer in the middle.

  • Small scope, real launch. We prefer a small site and one AI assistant that goes live over a big “platform” on slides.
  • Flexible tech. Any language or framework is possible as long as it is maintainable for the client.
  • Clear ownership. Domains, hosting, SSL and code are set up in the client’s name.
  • Light ongoing support. We stay available on WhatsApp, email and short calls for small tweaks and updates.
“We are not a big agency. We are engineers who like shipping real projects end-to-end websites, AI agents and small tools that fit what the client actually needs.”
